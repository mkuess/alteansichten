1. Context
This is a Laravel/Filament project. We simplified the Place create/edit form. However, latitude and longitude were removed from the form.

2. Problem
That is not correct for all places. Some places do not have a normal street address, for example a railway bridge, monument, wayside cross, old station area, viewpoint, or historic site. For those places, coordinates are necessary.

3. Goal
Add latitude and longitude back to the Place create/edit form, but make them optional and clearly separated from the normal address fields.

4. Requirements
- Re-add latitude and longitude fields to the Place form.
- The fields must be optional, not required.
- Do not require an address if latitude/longitude are provided.
- Do not require latitude/longitude if an address is provided.
- Keep coordinate accuracy and location hint removed for now.
- Do not re-add unnecessary period/date fields.
- Do not re-add the visible slug field.
- Do not change the database schema.
- Do not delete or rename database columns.
- Do not touch media item forms.
- Do not change upload logic.
- Do not change navigation, sidebar, dashboard, theme, CSS, or unrelated resources.

5. UX direction
Place the latitude and longitude fields in a small separate section, for example:
“Koordinaten / genaue Position”

The helper text should explain:
“Optional. Useful for places without a clear street address, such as bridges, monuments or historical locations.”

6. Validation
Use simple numeric validation for latitude and longitude if already appropriate.
If possible:
- latitude should be between -90 and 90
- longitude should be between -180 and 180

Do not add complex geocoding or map picker logic now.

7. Expected result
The Place form supports both cases:
- normal place with municipality/address
- special place without address but with coordinates

8. Stop condition
After the change, stop and summarize:
- which file was changed
- where latitude/longitude were added back
- confirmation that they are optional
- confirmation that no unrelated fields or logic were changed

Please stop afterward and do not implement any further steps without being asked.
